Deep Learning Specialization — Andrew Ng

Course Description

This specialization focuses on advanced artificial intelligence techniques using deep neural networks. It covers how modern AI systems are designed, trained, and optimized to solve real-world problems.

Students gain practical experience building deep learning models used in computer vision, natural language processing, and speech recognition. The course emphasizes optimization techniques, hyperparameter tuning, and scalable model deployment.

Instructor Details

Andrew Ng is a globally recognized leader in AI and deep learning education. His work has influenced millions of learners and professionals in the field.

Course Curriculum

  • Neural Networks and Backpropagation
  • Hyperparameter Tuning
  • Convolutional Neural Networks (CNNs)
  • Recurrent Neural Networks (RNNs)
  • Natural Language Processing
  • Deep Learning Applications

The program includes hands-on projects that simulate real-world AI challenges.

Duration

3–6 months
