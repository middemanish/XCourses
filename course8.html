Introduction to Artificial Intelligence (AI) — IBM

Course Description

This course introduces the fundamentals of artificial intelligence and its applications across industries. It provides a conceptual understanding of how AI systems are designed and used in real-world scenarios.

Students explore machine learning, neural networks, and AI ethics while understanding the impact of AI on society and business.

Instructor Details

The course is developed by IBM, a global leader in technology and artificial intelligence solutions.

Course Curriculum

  • AI Fundamentals
  • Machine Learning Basics
  • Neural Networks
  • AI Applications
  • Ethics in AI
  • Case Studies

The course is designed for beginners and provides a strong conceptual foundation.

Duration

4–6 weeks
