Machine Learning Specialization — Andrew Ng

Course Description

This specialization is designed to provide a strong foundation in machine learning, one of the most transformative technologies of the modern era. It introduces fundamental concepts such as supervised learning, unsupervised learning, and neural networks in a structured and intuitive manner.

Students learn how machines can extract patterns from data and use those patterns to make predictions and decisions. The course emphasizes both theoretical understanding and hands-on implementation using real-world datasets. It also introduces modern practices like model evaluation, error analysis, bias-variance tradeoff, and performance tuning.

Instructor Details

Andrew Ng is one of the most influential figures in artificial intelligence. He is the co-founder of Coursera and founder of DeepLearning.AI. He has led groundbreaking work in AI at Stanford University and Google Brain, and has educated millions of students worldwide.

Course Curriculum

  • Supervised Learning (Linear & Logistic Regression)
  • Unsupervised Learning (Clustering, Dimensionality Reduction)
  • Neural Networks Fundamentals
  • Decision Trees and Ensemble Techniques
  • Model Evaluation and Optimization
  • Real-world Applications like Recommendation Systems

The specialization consists of multiple courses that progressively build knowledge and practical expertise.

Duration

1–3 months (self-paced), typically 8–10 weeks
